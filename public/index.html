About

The Skyline Restaurant began in the year 2019 in the heart of Lagos, Nigeria; Ikeja. It began as a mini-bar which has grown to be what you see it to be now. All this was because of its impeccable customer service, sumptuous meals that leave the customers wanting more and How affordable and satisfying it was, and still is.

Skyline is an upscale casual restaurant that has never compromised on its fundamental promise to its clients. We provide great freshly made Nigerian cuisine from the northern, southern, western and eastern parts of the country.

A poolside is located behind the restaurant and it's provided as a place of relaxation. Tables are provided along the pool for those who would like to have their meals there.
